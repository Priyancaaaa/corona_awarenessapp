What are the symptoms of a coronavirus?

COVID-19 symptoms range from mild to severe. It takes 2-14 days after exposure for symptoms to develop.

Symptoms may include:

Fever

Cough

Shortness of breath

Chills

Shivering

Muscle pain

Headache

Sore throat

Taste Loss
